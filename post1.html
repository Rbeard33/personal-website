The Ultimate Guide to Peoria's Best Bike Trails

Whether you're an avid mountain biker or road cycling enthusiast, Peoria offers diverse trails and scenic routes for every skill level. This guide covers the best spots for both mountain and road biking, plus local favorite stops for post-ride refreshments.

Top Trails for Mountain Biking

Farmdale Reservoir: Known for its technical trails and natural terrain, this is a must-visit spot for mountain biking enthusiasts. With over 15 miles of trails ranging from beginner to advanced, you can expect everything from steep climbs to flowing descents.

Black Partridge Park: Ideal for intermediate riders, this park features scenic woodland trails and fun obstacles. Its well-maintained tracks make it a local favorite.

Scenic Routes for Road Biking

Rock Island Greenway: This 13-mile paved trail connects several parks and neighborhoods, offering a smooth ride perfect for road bikers. Enjoy the beautiful views of Peoria's greenery along the way.

Grandview Drive: Dubbed "the most beautiful drive in Illinois," this route is a dream for cyclists. It combines gentle inclines with stunning views of the Illinois River, making it ideal for casual and seasoned riders alike.
