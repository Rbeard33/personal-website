Introduction

Whether you're into road biking or mountain biking, Peoria has some great trails and paths to offer. In this post, I’ll share my favorite bike routes, separated by terrain and bike type, along with some food spots perfect for grabbing a bite or a beer after your ride.

Best Spots for Mountain Bikes

If you're into off-road, rugged terrain, these are some of the best mountain biking spots around Peoria:

  • Farmdale Reservoir: This place is ideal for those who love challenging trails with some great elevation changes. The dirt trails are perfect for mountain biking, offering both beginner and advanced paths.
  • Independence Park: Known for its beautiful natural surroundings, this park has some excellent technical trails for mountain bikers. It’s a mix of gravel, dirt, and roots, offering a little bit of everything.
  • Wildlife Prairie Park: A hidden gem for mountain bikers, featuring well-maintained dirt trails through woods and prairies. It’s great for both beginners and intermediate riders.

Best Spots for Road Bikes

If smooth pavement and long stretches of open road are more your speed, check out these road biking spots:

  • Grandview Drive: This scenic road is one of the best places to ride a road bike in Peoria. It’s a smooth, easy ride with beautiful views of the Illinois River. Perfect for beginners or those looking for a relaxed ride.
  • Rock Island Greenway: A long, paved trail that stretches from Peoria Heights all the way to Morton. It's perfect for long-distance road biking, and you can easily clock 20+ miles in a single ride.
  • IL-29 River Road: This road runs along the Illinois River and offers a more challenging ride for experienced road bikers. It’s a great route if you’re looking to pick up speed and test your endurance.

Food and Beer Stops Along the Way

No ride is complete without a good stop for food or a refreshing drink. Here are some of my favorite spots to fuel up during or after a ride:

  • Rhythm Kitchen Music Café: Located in Peoria's Riverfront District, this is a perfect spot to grab a meal after a long ride. They offer great food, drinks, and live music on weekends.
  • Peoria Brewing Company: After a solid ride on the Rock Island Greenway, reward yourself with a craft beer at this local brewery. It’s got a laid-back atmosphere, and the beer selection is top-notch.
  • Hearth Restaurant: A great option for those riding near Grandview Drive. They offer fresh, local ingredients and are known for their pizza and salads – perfect for a post-ride meal.

If you're in the area, be sure to check out these trails and spots. Whether you're a road biker or mountain biker, there's something for everyone in Peoria. Happy riding!
